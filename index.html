Privacy Policy

1. The "We Don't Collect Data" Rule

Ziel: No Excuse System is a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data or habit entries. All of your data is saved securely and locally on your own device.

2. Third Party Services

To process premium subscriptions and lifetime purchases, we use Google Play Billing and RevenueCat. These services may collect anonymous identifiers and purchase history to validate your transactions. They do not have access to your habit data.
