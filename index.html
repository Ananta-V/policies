Data We Collect

User input including tasks, habits, journal entries, health logs, schedules, and reminders. Profile information you provide such as name, age, mission, and an optional profile photo. App settings and preferences. If you activate premium: limited entitlement data — your license key, purchase token, and device ID.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to provide app features on your device. When you activate a license or make a purchase, limited data is used only to verify your premium access.

Data Storage

App data is stored in an on-device SQLite database. Sensitive items — including PIN hash, device ID, license proofs, and attempt counters — are stored in secure storage on your device. Nothing is written to external servers without your action.

Sharing & Selling

We do not share or sell your content. The only limited data transmission is for premium verification: a license key or purchase token and device ID are sent to Supabase and/or Google Play solely for validation.

User-Controlled Exports

If you export a report or share a PDF or image, the data is sent only to the destination you choose. No automatic uploads occur.

App Access

Login

No login required to use Ziel.

Test Account

Not required.

PIN Lock (Optional)

Enable an optional PIN lock via Settings › PIN Security. When enabled, the app locks whenever you leave the active state — pressing the power button or switching apps. Enter your PIN to unlock. To disable: Settings › PIN Security › Disable PIN.

Premium Access

No account needed. Go to Profile › License to activate with a token, or tap Upgrade with Google Play on Android. Without a license, Ziel remains fully usable in free mode.
