Privacy Policy

Local-First Architecture

Ziel: No Excuse System is a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data, habit entries, or usage statistics. All of your habit data is saved securely and locally on your own device's storage using SQLite and on-device secure storage. We do not operate any servers that receive or read your habit data.

Data We Store On Your Device

The following data is created by you and stored only on your device:

• Tasks, habits, journal entries, health logs, schedules, and reminders you create.
• Profile information you provide — name, age, mission statement, and an optional profile photo.
• App settings and preferences.
• PIN lock hash (stored in secure storage — never transmitted).
• Premium entitlement cache from RevenueCat (stored locally by the SDK).

Third-Party Services for Payment

To process premium subscriptions and one-time lifetime purchases, we use RevenueCat and Google Play Billing. When you make or restore a purchase, these services may collect:

• An anonymous RevenueCat user identifier (randomly generated, not tied to your name or email).
• Your Google Play purchase token, used solely to validate your entitlement.
• Device information required by the Google Play SDK for fraud prevention.

These services do not have access to your habit data, journal entries, profile photo, or any other content you create inside the app. Their data collection is strictly limited to what is necessary for payment processing.

• RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
• Google Play Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

User-Controlled Exports

When you choose to export a report or share a PDF or image, the data is sent only to the destination you explicitly choose (e.g. your own gallery, a messaging app). No automatic uploads occur. Ziel does not upload your exports to any server.

No Analytics or Advertising

Ziel does not contain analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising networks, or any tracking libraries. We do not collect usage data such as which screens you visit or how often you open the app.

Children's Privacy

Ziel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because all data remains on-device, there is no mechanism for us to receive data from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

App Access

Login

No login, account, or email address is required to use Ziel.

Test Account

No test account is required. The app is fully usable without any credentials.

PIN Lock (Optional)

You can enable an optional PIN lock via Settings › PIN Security. When enabled, the app locks whenever it leaves the active state — pressing the power button or switching apps will lock the screen. Enter your PIN to unlock. To disable: Settings › PIN Security › Disable PIN.

Your PIN is stored as a hash in secure on-device storage. It is never transmitted.

Premium Access

No account is needed for premium. Tap Profile › Upgrade to start a 7-day free trial via Google Play (monthly subscription) or purchase lifetime access. Your entitlement is verified by RevenueCat using your Google Play account.

Purchases can be restored at any time from the Profile screen → Restore purchases. Subscription management (cancel, pause, etc.) is handled through your Google Play account.
